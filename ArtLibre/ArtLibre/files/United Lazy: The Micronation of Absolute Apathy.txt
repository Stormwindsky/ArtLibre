National Motto

    "Why do today what you can put off until tomorrow... or never."

Political System: Absolute Lazinessism

    Description: A political doctrine based on minimal effort, indefinite postponement, and a complete lack of structure. Lazinessism is the only ideology, and it mandates doing nothing at all.

    Government: There is none. Creating a government would require effort. Consequently, United Lazy is an Anarchy of Apathy, where no laws are passed, and no decrees are signed, because no one can be bothered to start the process.

    Head of State: The Grand Absentee (or the Supreme Effort Disapprover). The title is purely symbolic and belongs to whoever is too lazy to claim it. It is usually represented by an empty sofa or a grease stain on a t-shirt.

    Hypothetical Election Campaign Slogan: "Don't vote, it's exhausting."

Territory and Geography

    Territory Name: The Non-Territory or the Un-Demarcated Zone of Indolence.

    Location: Everywhere and Nowhere. The territory is officially defined as "the 30 square centimeters of fabric under the back of a reclining citizen." United Lazy is a soil-less nation because drawing borders is far too much effort.

    Capital: Non-Applicable. Suggesting a capital is a waste of energy. If one had to be chosen, it would be The Bed.

Economy and Currency

    Economic System: Intermittent Barter. Transactions only occur if both parties are too lazy to argue about the offer.

    Currency: The Misplaced Coin (M-C). Its value is based on the minimum amount of movement required to exchange it. Credit card transactions are forbidden: typing a PIN code is excessive effort.

    Taxes: Uncollected. Creating a tax administration is beyond the strength of anyone.

Culture and National Symbols

    Flag: A solid-colored rectangle (dull grey) to minimize design effort. If possible, the flag will never be hoisted (too much effort) but merely crumpled in a corner.

    National Anthem: A deep silence, because singing would be too tiring. If a noise must be made, it should be an audible sigh or a simple "meh...".

    National Sport: Couch Channel-Surfing or Synchronized Napping. The only goal is to break the world record for immobility.

    Gastronomy: The Ready-to-Eat Microwave Meal (RTEMM). Utensils are forbidden (washing up), and one eats directly from the original container (even if it's hot, too lazy to wait).

Fundamental Lazinessist Quotes

    "Procrastination is not a problem, it's a policy."

    "Never get up before noon if you can roll to the kitchen."

    "Problems will solve themselves... or not. We'll see."
